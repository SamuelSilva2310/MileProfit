

# Uber / TVDE earnings calculator platform

Description: 

A mobile platform designed for TVDE drivers to track earnings, monitor vehicle usage, manage expenses, and understand real profitability.

Drivers working with ride-hailing platforms such as Uber and Bolt often struggle to understand their real income after fuel, electricity, maintenance, and platform fees. This app centralizes financial and operational data to provide clear insights into daily, weekly, and monthly performance.

The platform allows drivers to log kilometers driven, earnings per platform, and operational costs, automatically calculating net income and performance metrics.

The goal is to help drivers run their activity like a business, not just track rides.


# Core Features

## 1. Driver Account System

Drivers access the platform through a personal account.

Initial version:
	•	Accounts created by the platform admin
	•	Secure login system
	•	Each account linked to a specific vehicle

Future improvements:
	•	Self-registration
	•	Multi-vehicle support
	•	Driver profile with vehicle details


## 2. Daily Activity Tracking

Drivers can log their workday activity.

Fields include:
	•	Start odometer (km)
	•	End odometer (km)
	•	Total distance automatically calculated
	•	Work start time
	•	Work end time

Outputs:
	•	Daily distance driven
	•	Weekly and monthly mileage summaries
	•	Average kilometers per working day



## 3. Earnings Tracking

Drivers can record earnings per platform.

Supported platforms may include:
	•	Uber
	•	Bolt
	•	Other ride-hailing or delivery platforms

Each entry includes:
	•	Date
	•	Platform
	•	Total earnings
	•	Platform commission (optional)
	•	Tips
	•	Bonuses / incentives

Outputs:
	•	Daily earnings
	•	Weekly / monthly totals
	•	Earnings per platform
	•	Earnings per kilometer
	•	Earnings per hour



## 4. Expense Management

Drivers can record all operational costs.

Expense categories:

Fuel / Charging
	•	Station name
	•	Fuel type or electricity
	•	Price per liter or kWh
	•	Total cost
	•	Quantity

Vehicle Maintenance

Examples:
	•	Oil changes
	•	Tires
	•	Brake pads
	•	Mechanical repairs

Vehicle Improvements

Examples:
	•	Phone mounts
	•	Dashcams
	•	Interior upgrades

Operational Costs

Examples:
	•	Car wash
	•	Cleaning materials
	•	Parking
	•	Tolls

Outputs:
	•	Monthly expense breakdown
	•	Cost per kilometer


## 5. Profit Calculation

The platform automatically calculates:

Gross Earnings
– Expenses
= Net Profit

Insights provided:
	•	Daily profit
	•	Weekly profit
	•	Monthly profit
	•	Cost per km
	•	Profit per km
	•	Profit per hour

This gives drivers a real understanding of how profitable their work actually is.


## 6. Analytics Dashboard

A visual dashboard showing key metrics:
	•	Total earnings
	•	Total expenses
	•	Net profit
	•	Distance driven
	•	Average earnings per km
	•	Average earnings per hour

Drivers can filter data by:
	•	Day
	•	Week
	•	Month
	•	Custom date range
